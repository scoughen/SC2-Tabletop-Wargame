Faction Overview

Core Philosophy

  • Flexibility: Buildings can relocate, add-ons provide choices
  • Repair: SCVs can repair mechanical units and buildings
  • Mobile Production: Most production buildings can lift off and move

  • Barracks: Unlocks Marauders

    • Barracks: 8 infantry/round
    • Factory: 4 vehicles/round
    • Starport: 2 air units/round
  • Can only produce basic units
