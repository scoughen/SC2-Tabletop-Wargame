Keywords Glossary

Unit Type Keywords

Armored: Vulnerable to Strike: Armored attacks. Heavy armor classification.

Biological: Can be healed by Medivacs. Affected by biological-specific abilities. Organic units.

Flying: Ignores terrain, buildings, units for movement. Can only be attacked by anti-air weapons.

Light: Vulnerable to Strike: Light attacks. Lightly armored classification.

Massive: Heaviest unit classification. Very large units (Ultralisks, Thors, Colossi).

Mechanical: Can be repaired by SCVs (Terran). Immune to biological effects. Robotic units.

Psionic: Rare classification for caster units. Specific to certain abilities.

Combat Keywords

Strike: [Type]: Unit gains +1 die per model vs specified type. Strike hits ignore armor saves.

Splash Damage: Attack hits multiple targets. Place template; all units within take automatic hits.

To Hit: Target number required on d6 to score hit. Lower is better (3+ better than 5+).

Armor Save: Target number required on d6 to prevent damage. Lower is better. Capped at 3+ maximum.

Special Abilities

Burrow (Zerg): Use ABILITY order to burrow/unburrow. Cannot be targeted without detection. Cannot move or attack while burrowed (except with Tunneling Claws).

Charge (Zealot): Once per activation, move +6" directly toward nearest enemy and engage in melee.

Blink (Stalker): Once per activation, teleport up to 8", ignoring terrain, units, buildings.

Cliff Jumping (Reaper, Colossus): Ignore elevation changes. Move over cliffs as if flat ground.

Cloak: Unit cannot be targeted without detection. Requires detector units/buildings to reveal.

Detection: Reveals cloaked/burrowed units within range. Raven, Overseer, Observer (units). Missile Turret, Spore Crawler, Photon Cannon (buildings).

Overwatch: Units on HOLD order can react when enemies move within LOS and range. Shoot at -1 To Hit before enemy completes action.

Regeneration (Zerg): Heal wounds during Production Phase if took no damage that round.

Repair (Terran SCV): Heal 1 wound on mechanical unit/building. Costs 5 Minerals. Must be adjacent.

Shield Regeneration (Protoss): Regenerate 1 shield per Production Phase. Units: only if took no damage. Buildings: automatic.

Stim Pack: Marines/Marauders gain +1 attack and +2" move for activation. Lose 1 wound. Cannot use if at 1 wound (unless Combat Shield).

Building Keywords

Automated Defense: Defensive structures attack during both players' turns. Overwatch always active with no -1 To Hit penalty.

Creep (Zerg): Zerg buildings must be on creep to function. Spreads 6" radius from Hatchery/Creep Tumor.

Garrison (Bunker): Can hold up to 4 infantry. Garrisoned units cannot be attacked. Fire at full range with +1 To Hit.

Lift Off (Terran): Building can use ABILITY order to lift off or land. Moves 6" while flying. Cannot produce while flying.

Pylon Power (Protoss): Buildings must be within 9" of Pylon to function. Unpowered buildings cannot produce or attack.

Warp In (Protoss): Probe starts construction then leaves. Building auto-completes after 1 round.

Warp Gate (Protoss): Instant unit production anywhere within 9" of any Pylon. No production queue.

Production Keywords

Command Group: Group of up to 12 combat units or up to 3 workers. Each group = 1 die in activation bag.

Larva (Zerg): All Zerg units cost larva + resources. Hatcheries generate 3 larva per Production Phase (max 9 stored).

Reactor (Terran): Double production capacity. Can only produce basic units.

Tech Lab (Terran): Unlocks advanced units. Can produce both basic and advanced units.
