# SC2-Tabletop-Wargame
SC2-based Tabletop Wargame Rules and Related Documents


A complete tabletop wargame adaptation of StarCraft 2, featuring all three factions (Terran, Protoss, and Zerg) with detailed rules, unit stats, and multiple gameplay scenarios.

## üéØ Scenarios

1. **Annihilation** - Destroy the enemy base (45-60 min, Low complexity)
2. **Control Points** - Capture and hold objectives (50-60 min, Medium complexity)
3. **Resource Rush** - Race to 1000 minerals (45-55 min, Medium complexity)
4. **Assassination** - Eliminate the enemy hero (30-45 min, Medium complexity)
5. **Hold the Line** - Asymmetric defender vs attacker (50-60 min, High complexity)
6. **Escalation** - Progressive tech phases (60-75 min, High complexity)
